Cybersecurity Analysis

For this project, I conducted a comprehensive cybersecurity assessment for an organization. The aim was to identify potential vulnerabilities and provide recommendations to enhance their security posture.

I performed penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, and evaluated existing security policies. Based on the findings, I provided a detailed report outlining the risks and proposed solutions to mitigate them.

Technologies Used:

  • Kali Linux
  • Nmap
  • Metasploit
